- **Difficulty:** 2 / 5
- **Learning objective:** Sort timestamps from newest to oldest.

## Task

## Result requirements

- **Ordering:** Order by ordered_at descending, then order_id descending.
- **Duplicates:** Duplicates are significant.

Write one PostgreSQL `SELECT` statement in `answers/03-order-by/q013.sql`. Do not modify the schema or data.
