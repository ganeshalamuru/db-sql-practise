# Q066 - EXISTS

- **Difficulty:** 3 / 5
- **Learning objective:** Filter parent rows by the existence of a related row.

## Task

Return customer_id, email, first_name, and last_name for customers who have placed at least one order.

## Result requirements

- **Ordering:** Order by customer_id ascending.
- **Duplicates:** Duplicates are significant.

Write one PostgreSQL `SELECT` statement in `answers/14-exists/q066.sql`. Do not modify the schema or data.
