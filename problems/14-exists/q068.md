# Q068 - EXISTS

- **Difficulty:** 4 / 5
- **Learning objective:** Test for a related row that satisfies an additional condition.

## Task

Return supplier_id, supplier_name, product_id, product_name, and unit_price for active products from suppliers that also supply at least one other active product priced above 1,000.00.

## Result requirements

- **Ordering:** Order by supplier_id ascending, then product_id ascending.
- **Duplicates:** Duplicates are significant.

Write one PostgreSQL `SELECT` statement in `answers/14-exists/q068.sql`. Do not modify the schema or data.
