# Q063 - SUBQUERIES

- **Difficulty:** 4 / 5
- **Learning objective:** Filter parent rows using a subquery that aggregates related rows.

## Task

Return customer_id, email, first_name, and last_name for customers who have placed more orders than the average number of orders per customer. Treat the average as the total number of orders divided by the number of distinct customers who placed an order.

## Result requirements

- **Ordering:** Order by customer_id ascending.
- **Duplicates:** Duplicates are significant.

Write one PostgreSQL `SELECT` statement in `answers/13-subqueries/q063.sql`. Do not modify the schema or data.
