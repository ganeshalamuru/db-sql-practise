# Q039 - HAVING

- **Difficulty:** 4 / 5
- **Learning objective:** Combine WHERE and HAVING so row filters and group filters occur at the correct stages.

## Task

Return supplier_id and active_product_count for suppliers with at least 10 active products. Exclude inactive products before forming groups.

## Result requirements

- **Ordering:** Order by active_product_count descending, then supplier_id ascending.
- **Duplicates:** Duplicates are significant.

Write one PostgreSQL `SELECT` statement in `answers/08-having/q039.sql`. Do not modify the schema or data.
