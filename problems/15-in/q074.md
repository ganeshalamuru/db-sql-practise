# Q074 - IN

- **Difficulty:** 5 / 5
- **Learning objective:** Combine IN, joins, grouping, and HAVING to filter aggregated results.

## Task

Return category_id, category_name, and active_product_count for categories that contain at least one active product reviewed by a customer from India. Include only categories with at least two active products.

## Result requirements

- **Ordering:** Order by active_product_count descending, then category_id ascending.
- **Duplicates:** Duplicates are significant.

Write one PostgreSQL `SELECT` statement in `answers/15-in/q074.sql`. Do not modify the schema or data.
