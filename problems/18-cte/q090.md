# Q090 - CTE

- **Difficulty:** 5 / 5
- **Learning objective:** Use multiple CTEs to compare grouped business metrics to an overall benchmark.

## Task

Return customer_id, email, first_name, last_name, delivered_order_count, and delivered_item_total for customers whose delivered_item_total is above the average delivered_item_total among customers with at least one delivered order. delivered_item_total is the sum of quantity multiplied by unit_price for delivered orders.

## Result requirements

- **Ordering:** Order by delivered_item_total descending, then customer_id ascending.
- **Duplicates:** Duplicates are significant.

Write one PostgreSQL `SELECT` statement in `answers/18-cte/q090.sql`. Do not modify the schema or data.
