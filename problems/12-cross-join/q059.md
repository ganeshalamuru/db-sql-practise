# Q059 - CROSS JOIN

- **Difficulty:** 5 / 5
- **Learning objective:** Use a CROSS JOIN to construct a filtered allocation grid.

## Task

Return warehouse_id, warehouse_code, product_id, sku, and unit_price for every warehouse-and-product combination where the product is active and priced at least 1,000.00.

## Result requirements

- **Ordering:** Order by warehouse_id ascending, then product_id ascending.
- **Duplicates:** Duplicates are significant.

Write one PostgreSQL `SELECT` statement in `answers/12-cross-join/q059.sql`. Do not modify the schema or data.
