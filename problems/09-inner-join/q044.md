# Q044 - INNER JOIN

- **Difficulty:** 4 / 5
- **Learning objective:** Combine three tables to enrich an event with related names.

## Task

Return review_id, product_name, customer_id, customer_first_name, customer_last_name, rating, and created_at for reviews with a rating of 4 or 5.

## Result requirements

- **Ordering:** Order by created_at descending, then review_id descending.
- **Duplicates:** Duplicates are significant.

Write one PostgreSQL `SELECT` statement in `answers/09-inner-join/q044.sql`. Do not modify the schema or data.
