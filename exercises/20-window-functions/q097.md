# Q097 - WINDOW FUNCTIONS

- **Difficulty:** 5 / 5
- **Learning objective:** Rank aggregated related-table metrics within a partition while retaining rows with zero activity.

## Task

Return category_id, category_name, product_id, sku, delivered_quantity, and delivered_quantity_rank for every active product. delivered_quantity is the total quantity of that product in delivered orders, including 0 for products with no delivered sales. delivered_quantity_rank is the dense rank within the product's category, with the highest delivered_quantity ranked 1 and ties sharing a rank.

## Result requirements

- **Ordering:** Order by category_id ascending, delivered_quantity_rank ascending, then product_id ascending.
- **Duplicates:** Duplicates are significant.

Write one PostgreSQL `SELECT` statement in `exercises/20-window-functions/q097.sql`. Do not modify the schema or data.
