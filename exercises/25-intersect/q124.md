# Q124 - INTERSECT

- **Difficulty:** 5 / 5
- **Learning objective:** Intersect location populations and combine multiple aggregate measures for the shared countries.

## Task

Return country_id, country_code, country_name, warehouse_count, store_count, and warehouse_capacity_units for countries that host at least one warehouse and at least one store. warehouse_capacity_units is the sum of capacity_units across the country's warehouses.

## Result requirements

- **Ordering:** Order by country_code ascending.
- **Duplicates:** Duplicates are significant.

Write one PostgreSQL `SELECT` statement in `exercises/25-intersect/q124.sql`. Do not modify the schema or data.
