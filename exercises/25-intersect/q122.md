# Q122 - INTERSECT

- **Difficulty:** 5 / 5
- **Learning objective:** Find customers shared by transaction and feedback populations before calculating their metrics.

## Task

Return customer_id, email, order_count, review_count, last_ordered_at, and last_reviewed_at for customers who have both placed at least one order and submitted at least one review.

## Result requirements

- **Ordering:** Order by customer_id ascending.
- **Duplicates:** Duplicates are significant.

Write one PostgreSQL `SELECT` statement in `exercises/25-intersect/q122.sql`. Do not modify the schema or data.
