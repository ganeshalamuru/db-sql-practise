# Q121 - INTERSECT

- **Difficulty:** 5 / 5
- **Learning objective:** Intersect sales and review populations, then join independent aggregate metrics.

## Task

Return product_id, sku, delivered_order_count, and review_count for active products that both appear in at least one delivered order and have at least one review. delivered_order_count is the number of distinct delivered orders containing the product.

## Result requirements

- **Ordering:** Order by delivered_order_count descending, review_count descending, then product_id ascending.
- **Duplicates:** Duplicates are significant.

Write one PostgreSQL `SELECT` statement in `exercises/25-intersect/q121.sql`. Do not modify the schema or data.
