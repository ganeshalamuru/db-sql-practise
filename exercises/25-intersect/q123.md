# Q123 - INTERSECT

- **Difficulty:** 5 / 5
- **Learning objective:** Intersect product availability sets from two inventory channels and retain channel-specific counts.

## Task

Return product_id, sku, warehouse_count, and store_count for active products with positive quantity_on_hand in at least one warehouse and at least one store. warehouse_count and store_count are the numbers of locations with positive quantity for the product in each channel.

## Result requirements

- **Ordering:** Order by product_id ascending.
- **Duplicates:** Duplicates are significant.

Write one PostgreSQL `SELECT` statement in `exercises/25-intersect/q123.sql`. Do not modify the schema or data.
