# Q062 - SUBQUERIES

- **Difficulty:** 4 / 5
- **Learning objective:** Use a correlated subquery to compare a row with the average of its group.

## Task

Return product_id, product_name, category_id, and unit_price for active products priced above the average unit_price of active products in the same category.

## Result requirements

- **Ordering:** Order by category_id ascending, unit_price descending, then product_id ascending.
- **Duplicates:** Duplicates are significant.

Write one PostgreSQL `SELECT` statement in `exercises/13-subqueries/q062.sql`. Do not modify the schema or data.
