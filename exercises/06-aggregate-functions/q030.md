# Q030 - Aggregate Functions

- **Difficulty:** 4 / 5
- **Learning objective:** Combine date filtering with timestamp and numeric aggregates.

## Task

For orders placed during 2024, return first_ordered_at, last_ordered_at, order_count, and average_shipping_fee. Round the average shipping fee to two decimal places.

## Result requirements

- **Ordering:** The result has one row; no ordering is needed.
- **Duplicates:** Duplicates are significant.

Write one PostgreSQL `SELECT` statement in `exercises/06-aggregate-functions/q030.sql`. Do not modify the schema or data.
