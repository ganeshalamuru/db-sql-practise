# Q117 - UNION

- **Difficulty:** 5 / 5
- **Learning objective:** Combine heterogeneous business locations while preserving type-specific attributes.

## Task

Return country_code, city_name, location_type, location_code, location_name, capacity_units, and employee_count for every warehouse and store. For warehouses, include capacity_units and the number of employees assigned to the warehouse. For stores, capacity_units and employee_count must be NULL. location_type is warehouse or store.

## Result requirements

- **Ordering:** Order by country_code ascending, location_type ascending, then location_code ascending.
- **Duplicates:** Duplicates are significant.

Write one PostgreSQL `SELECT` statement in `exercises/24-union/q117.sql`. Do not modify the schema or data.
