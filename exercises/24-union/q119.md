# Q119 - UNION

- **Difficulty:** 5 / 5
- **Learning objective:** Use a set union to deduplicate products that meet either of two availability rules.

## Task

Return product_id, sku, and name for active products that are available in at least one warehouse with positive quantity_on_hand or in at least one store with positive quantity_on_hand. Include each qualifying product once.

## Result requirements

- **Ordering:** Order by product_id ascending.
- **Duplicates:** Duplicates are significant.

Write one PostgreSQL `SELECT` statement in `exercises/24-union/q119.sql`. Do not modify the schema or data.
