# Q116 - UNION

- **Difficulty:** 5 / 5
- **Learning objective:** Unify entities with different relationship paths into a normalized contact directory.

## Task

Return email, contact_name, contact_type, and country_code for every customer and supplier. contact_name is the customer's first and last name joined with one space, or the supplier name. contact_type is customer or supplier. A customer country is determined through the customer's city; a supplier country is determined directly. Include all rows from both entity types.

## Result requirements

- **Ordering:** Order by contact_type ascending, then email ascending.
- **Duplicates:** Duplicates are significant.

Write one PostgreSQL `SELECT` statement in `exercises/24-union/q116.sql`. Do not modify the schema or data.
