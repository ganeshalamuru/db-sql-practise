# Q040 - HAVING

- **Difficulty:** 5 / 5
- **Learning objective:** Apply multiple aggregate conditions to retain qualifying groups.

## Task

Return product_id, review_count, and average_rating for products that have at least 5 reviews and an average review rating of at least 4.00. Round average_rating to two decimal places.

## Result requirements

- **Ordering:** Order by average_rating descending, then review_count descending, then product_id ascending.
- **Duplicates:** Duplicates are significant.

Write one PostgreSQL `SELECT` statement in `exercises/08-having/q040.sql`. Do not modify the schema or data.
