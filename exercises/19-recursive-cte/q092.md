# Q092 - RECURSIVE CTE

- **Difficulty:** 3 / 5
- **Learning objective:** Use a recursive CTE to list all direct and indirect reports of one employee.

## Task

Return employee_id, first_name, last_name, manager_id, and hierarchy_level for employee_id 1 and every employee who reports to them directly or indirectly. hierarchy_level is 0 for employee_id 1.

## Result requirements

- **Ordering:** Order by hierarchy_level ascending, then employee_id ascending.
- **Duplicates:** Duplicates are significant.

Write one PostgreSQL `SELECT` statement in `exercises/19-recursive-cte/q092.sql`. Do not modify the schema or data.
