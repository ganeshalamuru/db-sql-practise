# Q094 - RECURSIVE CTE

- **Difficulty:** 5 / 5
- **Learning objective:** Combine recursive traversal with joins and filtering on related business data.

## Task

Return employee_id, first_name, last_name, hierarchy_level, warehouse_code, and warehouse_name for every direct or indirect report of employee_id 1 who works in a warehouse located in India. Exclude employee_id 1.

## Result requirements

- **Ordering:** Order by hierarchy_level ascending, then employee_id ascending.
- **Duplicates:** Duplicates are significant.

Write one PostgreSQL `SELECT` statement in `exercises/19-recursive-cte/q094.sql`. Do not modify the schema or data.
