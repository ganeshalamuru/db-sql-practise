# Q127 - EXCEPT

- **Difficulty:** 5 / 5
- **Learning objective:** Subtract feedback participants from purchasers before summarizing purchase history.

## Task

Return customer_id, email, order_count, and last_ordered_at for customers who have placed at least one order but have never submitted a review.

## Result requirements

- **Ordering:** Order by last_ordered_at descending, then customer_id ascending.
- **Duplicates:** Duplicates are significant.

Write one PostgreSQL `SELECT` statement in `exercises/26-except/q127.sql`. Do not modify the schema or data.
