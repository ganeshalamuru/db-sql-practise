# Q129 - EXCEPT

- **Difficulty:** 5 / 5
- **Learning objective:** Subtract suppliers associated with returned active products from the active supplier population.

## Task

Return supplier_id, supplier_name, active_product_count, and average_active_unit_price for suppliers that have at least one active product but no active product appearing in returns.

## Result requirements

- **Ordering:** Order by average_active_unit_price descending, then supplier_id ascending.
- **Duplicates:** Duplicates are significant.

Write one PostgreSQL `SELECT` statement in `exercises/26-except/q129.sql`. Do not modify the schema or data.
