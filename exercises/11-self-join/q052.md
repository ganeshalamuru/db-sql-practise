# Q052 - SELF JOIN

- **Difficulty:** 4 / 5
- **Learning objective:** Use a LEFT self join to retain hierarchy roots.

## Task

Return employee_id, employee_first_name, employee_last_name, manager_first_name, and manager_last_name for every employee, including employees who have no manager.

## Result requirements

- **Ordering:** Order by employee_id ascending.
- **Duplicates:** Duplicates are significant.

Write one PostgreSQL `SELECT` statement in `exercises/11-self-join/q052.sql`. Do not modify the schema or data.
