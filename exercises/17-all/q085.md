# Q085 - ALL

- **Difficulty:** 5 / 5
- **Learning objective:** Use ALL with grouping and a correlated aggregate subquery.

## Task

Return supplier_id, supplier_name, product_id, product_name, and unit_price for active products whose unit_price is at least as high as every other active product from the same supplier. Include only suppliers with at least two active products, and include ties.

## Result requirements

- **Ordering:** Order by supplier_id ascending, then product_id ascending.
- **Duplicates:** Duplicates are significant.

Write one PostgreSQL `SELECT` statement in `exercises/17-all/q085.sql`. Do not modify the schema or data.
