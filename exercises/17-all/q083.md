# Q083 - ALL

- **Difficulty:** 4 / 5
- **Learning objective:** Use a correlated ALL subquery to find a group maximum.

## Task

Return product_id, product_name, category_id, and unit_price for active products whose unit_price is at least as high as every other active product in the same category. Include ties.

## Result requirements

- **Ordering:** Order by category_id ascending, then product_id ascending.
- **Duplicates:** Duplicates are significant.

Write one PostgreSQL `SELECT` statement in `exercises/17-all/q083.sql`. Do not modify the schema or data.
