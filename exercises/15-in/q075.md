# Q075 - IN

- **Difficulty:** 5 / 5
- **Learning objective:** Use a nested subquery to filter a multi-table transaction result.

## Task

Return order_id, ordered_at, customer_id, customer_first_name, customer_last_name, and order_item_count for orders placed by customers who have also submitted at least one review with a rating of 5. Include only orders containing at least two line items.

## Result requirements

- **Ordering:** Order by ordered_at descending, then order_id descending.
- **Duplicates:** Duplicates are significant.

Write one PostgreSQL `SELECT` statement in `exercises/15-in/q075.sql`. Do not modify the schema or data.
